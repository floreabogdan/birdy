{{template "icon-info"}}How this works

The BGP Monitoring Protocol (RFC 7854) streams your sessions' route data and up/down events to a collector — a route-collector, an analytics pipeline, or a looking-glass backend. BIRD monitors every BGP session automatically; a station just says where the stream goes.

Pre-policy mirrors what a peer sent before your import filters ran; post-policy mirrors what survived them. Sending both lets the collector see exactly what your policies dropped.

BMP is a preliminary feature in BIRD (2.14+), and the daemon must be built with BMP support. birdy renders the config; bird -p on Changes is what confirms your build accepts it.

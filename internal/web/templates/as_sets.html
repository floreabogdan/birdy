{{template "icon-info"}}What an AS set is for

BIRD has no idea what an IRR AS-SET is. When a customer hands you AS-CUSTOMER, the usual practice is to expand it offline — with bgpq4 or similar — into the list of member AS numbers, and drop that list into the router config.

That list is what lives here. An import policy can then require that every route’s origin AS be a member, which is how you say “announce your own prefixes and your downstreams’, and nobody else’s”. Record the AS-SET name under Source so the expansion can be re-run later.
